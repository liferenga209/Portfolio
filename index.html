About Pacific Strategic Security Analysis Group (PSSAG)

The Pacific Strategic Security Analysis Group (PSSAG) is a non-government, independent research and security analysis organization based in Port Moresby, Papua New Guinea.

PSSAG focuses on intelligence research, security risk assessment, defense analysis, geopolitical monitoring, and strategic advisory services across the Pacific region.
